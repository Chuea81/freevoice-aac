What FreeVoice Is

FreeVoice AAC is a free, open-source augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) application. It is provided as-is, at no cost, with no warranty.

Not Medical Advice

FreeVoice is a communication tool, not a medical device. It is not a substitute for professional speech-language pathology services. Consult a licensed speech-language pathologist (SLP) for clinical AAC assessment and recommendations.

Privacy

FreeVoice collects zero data. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising. All data (boards, settings, voice preferences) is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. If you delete the app or clear browser data, all data is permanently gone. We have no copy.

Symbols

Pictographic symbols are provided by ARASAAC (Aragonese Portal of Augmentative and Alternative Communication), created by Sergio Palao for the Government of Aragon, Spain. Used under Creative Commons BY-NC-SA 4.0.

AI Voices

AI voice synthesis is powered by Kokoro TTS (Apache 2.0 license), running entirely on-device via WebAssembly. No audio data is ever transmitted to any server.
