FreeVoice AAC — Free Communication for Every Child

WHY FREEVOICE?

Most AAC apps cost $200–$300/year in subscriptions. Families with nonverbal children already spend thousands on therapies, equipment, and care. We decided that was wrong.

FreeVoice is completely free. Forever. No hidden fees, no credit card, no subscription trap.

WHAT IS AAC?

Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) gives nonverbal and minimally verbal children a voice. Our app lets them communicate with 1,100+ symbols, instantly speak sentences in 10 languages, and customize boards for their life.

CORE FEATURES

SPEECH BAR
Build sentences symbol-by-symbol, then speak them out loud. Every symbol shows a colorful emoji, text label, and category color (verb=green, noun=orange, etc). Tap to add, long-press to remove.

FAST PHRASES
12 critical social phrases always visible — "Help," "Wait," "Done," "More," "Stop," "Look," etc. One tap away, no board navigation.

SEARCH
Find any symbol instantly. Search by word or browse categories. Type or speak to find what you need.

CUSTOMIZATION
Add your own symbols using photos or emoji. Create custom boards for specific contexts (mealtimes, school days, favorite activities). Pin frequently-used symbols to the top.

VOICE
Choose from clear AI voices, your device's built-in voice, or record a family member's voice for a personal touch.

PARENT MODE
PIN-protected access to settings. Adjust symbol size, colors, voice speed, language — tailored to each child's needs.

BACKUP & SHARING
Export your boards as JSON to back up locally. Share boards via compressed URL with therapists or caregivers. Import community boards.
